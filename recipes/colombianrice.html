Colombian Coconut Rice

Description

Arroz con coco,is made from rice, coconut milk, water, sugar, salt, and raisins.

This dish is sure to meet your craving of something savoury and slightly sweet!

Ingredients

  • 14 oz can of coconut milk
  • 2 Tbsp of coconut oil
  • 2 cups of white rice
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 3 cups of water

Instructions

  1. In a wide saucepan, heat the coconut milk until simmering.Reduce the heat to medium low and simmer, stirring often, until the coconut milk has reduced to the point where it becomes a thick paste and the solid coconut pieces begin to turn a deep golden brown, 50-60 minutes.
  2. Once you have a golden brown coconut paste, add the rice, sugar, and salt. Turn the heat to medium high and continure to cook until the rice grains begin to turn golden brown, 3-4 minutes.
  3. Remove the pan from the heat and add water carefully. Stir to combine.
  4. Return the pan to the heat and bring the mixture to a simmer. Then reduce the heat to low, cover and cook for 15 minutes.
  5. Remove the rice from the heat and let rest for 10-15 minutes covered.
  6. Fluff with a fork before serving.
  7. Enjoy!
