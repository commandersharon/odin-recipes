Pumpkin Cold Foam

Description

Delicious pumpkin fall vibes in a drink! Easy to make, and cheaper than the drive thru. Simple and real ingredients are used in this recipe, including pumpkin.

This recipe is easy to make and you can store the leftovers in your fridge. Feel free to take it out of the fridge every morning and top your cup of Joe with it.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoon pumpkin puree
  • 2 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice
  • 2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream

Instructions

  1. Whisk together heavy whipping cream, sweetened condensed milk, and vanilla.
  2. Whisk in the pumpkin puree and pumpkin spice until smooth.
  3. Cover and place into a container.
  4. Lastly top off your coffee with it! (or eat it by itself no one will judge you)
